## How it all began

Earlier this May in 2026, I went off to Indonesia on my very first trip with the friends I made in Air Wing, OCS. They were my fellow instructors who took the batch of 143/26 — a batch that holds a special place in my heart, since they were my very first.

It was a wonderfully impromptu trip. Caleb settled the tickets, Rayson sorted out the insurance, and everyone else was just vibing along for the ride.

![The crew soaking in the view](/images/projects/project-01/soloaura.png)

## Our guide for five days

When we landed, we met up with our driver, who quickly became so much more than that — he was basically our tour guide for the next five days. He knew exactly where to take us and when, and made the whole experience feel effortless.

![Sunrise over Mount Bromo](/images/projects/project-01/mtbromo.png)

## Life on the road

Honestly, the journey itself was about 90% road trips and sleeping. But somehow, that was part of the charm. The long drives gave us time to talk, laugh, and just exist together away from the demands of work.

And the pictures we took along the way? Next level amazing — all thanks to Caleb's Fujifilm.

![Chasing waterfalls at Tumpak Sewu](/images/projects/project-01/sewufalls.png)

## A trip to remember

Overall, I'd say this was a trip to remember for a lifetime. Great people, stunning scenery, and the kind of spontaneous adventure you can't really plan for.

![Good vibes only](/images/projects/project-01/sunglass.jpg)
